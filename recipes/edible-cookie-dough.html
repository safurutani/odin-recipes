Edible Cookie Dough Recipe

About this recipe

Sometimes you need to treat yourself. I slightly tweaked a recipe that I found on instagram. I simply doubled it as the portion was not big enough to satiate my sweet tooth snacking needs. I also increased the amount of milk but not to any specific amount because the consistency can vary. It is easy enough to adjust to your liking.



Ingredients

  • 1 C flour
  • 2 Tbsp white sugar
  • 4 Tbsp brown sugar
  • pinch of salt
  • 2 Tbsp melted butter
  • 1/4 C milk
  • 1/2 Tsp vanilla extract
  • handful of chocolate chips


Instructions

  1. Microwave the flour for 30 seconds (optional)
  2. Mix both the white and brown sugar with the flour and get rid of any clumps
  3. Add the salt and melted butter
  4. Add the milk in with the vanilla extract and mix altogether
  5. You may want to add more milk if the dough isn't getting soft enough or it looks too dry still
  6. Finally add a nice handful of chocolate chips or however many makes your heart content!
