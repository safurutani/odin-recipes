Sweet Potato Gnocchi Recipe

About This Recipe

This recipe is cheap and has relatively few ingredients, allowing most anyone to do it. There is a good amount of prep involved, but it makes a fair amount. There is also plenty of room to go beyond the simple ingredients used to add your own flare and get even more flavors into the dish!



Ingredients

  • 2 sweet potatoes
  • pinch of salt
  • 2 C flour

  • parsley
  • garlic
  • walnuts
  • lemon juice
  • olive oil


Instructions

  1. Bake the sweet potatoes for 30 minutes at 350 F
  2. Once cool, scoop out the insides and mash them in a bowl
  3. Add a pinch of salt and all the flour and mix until it becomes thick and dough-like
  4. Knead the sweet potato dough into a ball on top of flour
  5. Divide it up into 3 smaller balls and roll them out into thin columns
  6. Cut each of the columns into bite-sized pieces
  7. Bring salted water to a boil and cook the gnocchi in it until they all float to the top
  8. While the gnocchi boil, finely chop your parsley, cut/mash your walnuts, and cut a few cloves of garlic
  9. Put the previous ingredients into a frying pan along with olive oil and some lemon juice to taste
  10. Once the gnocchi is done boiling, add them to the pan with the sauce and cook until it is nicely coated
